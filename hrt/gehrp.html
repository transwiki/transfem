In season 2 episode 13 of House M.D., a 15-year-old female fashion model collapses on stage and is eventually diagnosed with paraneoplastic syndrome secondary to an unknown cancer. However, the team is unable to actually find a tumor until House orders an MRI of her groin after piecing together a few anomalies from her medical history, like that she had never menstruated. Surprisingly, she is found to have testicular cancer and XY sex chromosomes. Now, this is House M.D. of course, the same show in which a comatose man is temporarily revived with an unproven injection of stimulants, taken to eat a sandwich, and then convinced to donate his heart to his dying son by committing suicide, but the intersex condition depicted in this episode does actually exist.
